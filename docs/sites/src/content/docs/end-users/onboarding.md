Use this checklist the first time you enter Allowealth.

## 1. Set your personal defaults

Go to **Profile** and confirm:

- Display name
- Locale and timezone
- Preferred currency display

This ensures dashboards and dates match your local context.

## 2. Connect your first account

Go to **Accounts** and add at least one account (bank, cash, wallet, or credit source).

Recommended starter setup:

- One daily spending account
- One savings account

## 3. Add your first 10 transactions

Go to **Transactions** and enter a representative week.

- Include both income and expense rows
- Assign categories as you add records
- Confirm totals on Dashboard match expectations

:::tip[💡 Tip]
Don’t wait for “perfect” data quality. Small realistic data early gives better budget signals than delayed imports.
:::

## 4. Review budget categories

Go to **Budget → Categories** and verify category list quality:

- Merge duplicates
- Archive categories you never use
- Set initial monthly limits on high-volume categories

## 5. Verify reports

Open **Reports** and confirm:

- Category split looks reasonable
- No large unknown categories remain
- Spending trend is visible for your seeded period
