Welcome to Allowealth. This guide helps you track expenses, manage budgets, and plan your financial future.

## What Allowealth Does

Allowealth organizes your financial life in one place:

- **Track transactions** - Record income and expenses as they happen
- **Manage accounts** - Monitor balances across cash, investments, and debt
- **Set budgets** - Plan spending limits by category and track progress
- **Analyze trends** - Review spending patterns with reports and charts
- **Plan ahead** - Forecast future wealth based on savings goals

## Start Here

New to Allowealth? Complete the [onboarding checklist](./onboarding/) to set up your workspace. This takes about 15 minutes and gives you a working budget baseline.

Already set up? Jump to the [daily workflow guide](./daily-workflow/) for routines that keep your data accurate.

## Quick Tips

- **Add transactions daily** - Five minutes each day prevents a backlog
- **Review budgets weekly** - Catch overspending before it compounds
- **Reconcile accounts monthly** - Match Allowealth balances with bank statements
- **Use categories consistently** - Clean categories produce accurate reports

Need help? Check the troubleshooting sections in each guide or contact your workspace administrator.
