Forecast shows where your current behavior could lead over time.

![Forecast screenshot placeholder](/images/user-guides/forecast-placeholder.svg)

## What you can do here

- Explore projected growth with visual trajectory charts.
- Adjust assumptions such as top-up amount, APY, and years.
- Review detailed period-by-period ledger projections.

## Scenario testing flow

1. Open **Forecast**.
2. Start with your realistic monthly contribution.
3. Adjust annual return assumptions conservatively.
4. Compare outcomes across different time horizons.
5. Save target assumptions in your own notes or planning process.

:::caution[⚠️ Caution]
Forecasts are estimates, not guarantees. Use conservative assumptions for planning decisions.
:::
