Accounts represent where your money and liabilities live.

![Accounts screenshot placeholder](/images/user-guides/accounts-placeholder.svg)

## What you can do here

- Create and edit accounts.
- Group accounts by class: liquid, non-liquid, and debt.
- Transfer value between accounts.
- View historical month snapshots.
- Manage account categories and closed accounts.

## Set up accounts the right way

1. Add your primary cash/bank account first.
2. Add savings or investment accounts next.
3. Add debt accounts (credit card/loan) so net position is realistic.
4. Verify account currency and category assignments.

:::note[📝 Note]
Historical month views are read-only. Use the current month view for updates.
:::

## Accuracy checklist

- Account totals match external statements.
- Debt accounts are classified correctly.
- Closed accounts are archived, not deleted, when you still need history.
