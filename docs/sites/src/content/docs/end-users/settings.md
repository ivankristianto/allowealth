Settings controls how your workspace behaves day to day.

![Settings screenshot placeholder](/images/user-guides/settings-placeholder.svg)

## Sections you will use

- **General**: Workspace name, currencies, week start, display preferences.
- **Members**: Invite members, review roles, and manage access.
- **Notifications**: Tune alert behavior.
- **Data**: High-impact actions like history clearing or reset workflows.

## Safe settings update checklist

1. Change one setting at a time.
2. Save and confirm the success message.
3. Verify downstream pages (Dashboard, Budget, Reports) still look correct.

:::caution[⚠️ Caution]
Data actions can be destructive. Confirm scope and backups before any reset or clear action.
:::

## Admin vs member behavior

- Admins can update workspace-wide configuration.
- Members can view settings but may have restricted edit permissions.
