## Compound interest walkthrough

1. Open **Calculators**.
2. Keep **Compound Interest** selected.
3. Enter principal, annual rate, and years.
4. Click **Calculate**.
5. Review projected outcome and compare alternatives.

:::note[📝 Note]
Use the same currency conventions as your workspace to keep calculations easy to compare with accounts and reports.
:::
