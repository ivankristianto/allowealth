Dashboard is your daily check-in screen.

![Dashboard screenshot placeholder](/images/user-guides/dashboard-placeholder.svg)

## What you can do here

- Review monthly spending, budget usage, and income.
- See recent transactions and account totals in one place.
- Finish onboarding tasks when your workspace is still being set up.

## Daily dashboard routine

1. Open **Dashboard**.
2. Check your current month spending card.
3. Scan recent transactions for obvious mistakes.
4. Open **Transactions** to fix issues right away.

:::note[📝 Note]
If onboarding is not complete, you will see checklist steps here until setup is finished.
:::

## When something looks wrong

- Spending seems too high: open **Transactions** and check duplicates.
- Account total seems off: open **Accounts** and review the latest balance updates.
- Budget status looks unusual: open **Budget** and inspect category-level limits.
